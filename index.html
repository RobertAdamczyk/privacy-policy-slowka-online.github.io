Privacy Policy for Słówka Online

Last updated: March 31, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Słówka Online. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our game.

2. Personal Data

We do not collect personal data from users.

We do not collect, store, or process information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Identifiers linked to a user's identity

3. Game Data

The only data processed by the app is game-related data, which is necessary for the online multiplayer gameplay to function.

This may include:

  • Moves made during gameplay
  • Current game state
  • Scores and turns
  • Temporary session and match data

This data:

  • Is used only to enable gameplay
  • Is not used to identify users
  • Is not used for analytics or advertising
  • Is not sold or shared with third parties

4. Data Storage

Game-related data may be stored on our servers only as long as necessary to maintain ongoing games and synchronize gameplay between players.

5. Third-Party Services

Słówka Online does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external services that collect personal data from users.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share user data with third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect personal data, the game is intended to be safe for users of all ages.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect game-related data processed by our servers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
